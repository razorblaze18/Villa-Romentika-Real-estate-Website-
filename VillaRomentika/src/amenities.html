At home with nature

At Villa Romentika our goal is to use natural light to create comfortable, beautiful spaces. Light is a very important part of design. We believe that the changes in the natural world throughout the day, through the season, through varying weather should be felt within the house. Care is taken to ensure that the harsh weather doesn't spoil your comfort, but by bringing the inhabitants closer to nature in their daily lives we attempt at creating a healthy abode.

KEY AMENITIES

  • Green Building Concepts for planning & construction. Energy efficient features for a sustained energy-efficient living.
  • Tremix concrete & heavy paver block internal road with ample street lights.
  • Impressive, single entry main entrance gate with security cabin.
  • Comprehensive Security system along the compound wall periphery.
  • Underground cabling for electricity, TV & Telephone for a wire-free look.
  • Rain water harvesting.
  • Plantation around and inside the premises.
  • Elevator for each villa (Optional)
